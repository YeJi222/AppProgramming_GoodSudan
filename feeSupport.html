The ongoing crisis in Sudan has been made worse by the pandemy of Covid-19, deteriorating economic climate,
recurrent acts of violence in numerous states, a meager harvest, and as well as
the changes in the price of grains and other food items on a global scale.
Moreover, with the removal of civic leaders from the military last year, the situation in Sudan is politically unsafe.
Therefore, let's help transforming lives in Sudan.



With the collected fund, we will help them to provide safety needs
through SOS Mayday Emergency and sponsor SOS villages.

This fund will also be used to provide the Sudanese for their daily needs.
