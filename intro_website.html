Purpose of Our Website

< For introduction >


Our website aims to give information about the problems in the country of Sudan, such as economic, political and educational crisis.

< For pray >


There is a separate page for praying for the Sudanese people. People around the world can see the difficulties that Sudanese people are currently experiencing through our web page and pray around the title of their prayer.

< For volunteering >


Our website is recruiting educational volunteers to provide quality education to Sudanese children. In addition to the 'Support page', the website serves as a bridge between volunteers and Sudanese people, including recruiting human resources for building buildings, emotional counselors for refugees, and volunteers with international driver's licenses to guide evacuation routes.
