Welcome all visitors to the Good Sudan website :)


In Sudan, there are already
about 500,000 kids
who has experienced severe acute
malnutrition every year.

The ongoing crisis has been made worse
by the deteriorating economic climate,
recurrent acts of violence in
numerous states, a meager harvest, and the
changes in the price of grains and other
food items on a global scale.

Also, it is politically unsafe, with the
removal of civic leaders from the military
last year.

Due to the continuing civil war, it is
difficult for children to receive education,
such as burning schools.
