Education support :)


Children in Sudan who have never experienced a safe daily life due to 15 years of civil war.
Nevertheless, in order to study, the children did not mind walking more than three hours to school.
Their parents are worried that they might lose their lives on the way to and from school and home.
But the desperation that you can't make your children's future your present was too great.


and, Unfortynately, as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic,
education systems worldwide have been elevastated.

Let's ensure access to education for children in Sudan suffering from
poverty, hunger, and civil war and provide quality educational opportunities together.





So that children who are marginalized by poverty, child labor, and discrimination
can dream of a better future Provide equal opportunities for quality education.

Improved access to education



Support educational facilities such as kindergartens, elementary, middle and high schools,
and educational equipment, It provides a high-quality educational environment
by providing tuition and scholarships.

Ensuring Quality Education



The quality of educational services is improved by supporting teachers and capacity building, cultivating literacy and math skills, and developing and operating various educational programs tailored to the development process such as gender and vocational education and training.

Strengthening awareness of educational rights



Conducting campaigns to defend educational rights, supporting self-governing activities,
Through the development and operation of lifelong education for parents and local residents,
Promote parents and communities' awareness of the right to children's education.
